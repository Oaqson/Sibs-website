Our Investment Thesis

We believe in founder-market fit and invest in people first. We look for these key traits in the founders we back.

1

Unwavering Passion

We back founders who are obsessed with solving a specific problem. This passion is the fuel that gets startups through the inevitable tough times.

Unique Insight

You see the world differently. You've identified a unique angle or an overlooked opportunity in a large, growing market.

3

Bias for Action

Great founders are builders. You move fast, learn quickly, and constantly iterate on your product and strategy based on real-world feedback.
